About me

My name is Hila, and I am a passionate and dedicated therapist based in Los Altos, California. Born and raised in Israel, I obtained my bachelor's and master's degrees in social work, with a specialization in trauma during my master's program.

Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of working in various settings, including mental health clinics, social services, schools and with at-risk youth. These experiences allowed me to develop a deep understanding of the impact of trauma on individuals' lives and the importance of providing support.

In 2012, I made the exciting move to California and obtained my professional license in the field. I joined a nonprofit organization, where I focused on assisting adults facing various challenges, with a particular emphasis on childhood trauma and its effects. The opportunity to work with individuals from diverse backgrounds and help them on their healing journeys has been truly rewarding.

Currently, I am thrilled to be running my private practice, where I continue to provide compassionate and personalized therapy to adults aged 18 and above, serving the Los Altos, Mountain View, Palo Alto and Sunnyvale area. I find immense fulfillment in connecting with people and supporting them through their personal struggles. Witnessing their growth and the positive changes they make in their lives is incredibly inspiring.

I truly love what I do and approach each therapeutic relationship with empathy, respect, and a commitment to creating a safe and non-judgmental space. Together, we will work collaboratively to address your unique needs and goals, and empower you to lead a more fulfilling life.

Thank you for visiting my website, and I look forward to the opportunity to connect with you and support you on your journey of healing and growth.

My therapeutic style

As a psychodynamic therapist, I bring a unique and compassionate approach to our sessions. My style is characterized by several key qualities that create a safe and nurturing space for your personal growth and healing:

Warmth: From the moment you step into our sessions, you'll find a warm and welcoming atmosphere. I believe that the foundation of effective therapy begins with a genuine connection and trust, and I am committed to creating an environment where you feel valued and understood.

Calm: In the midst of life's chaos, our therapy sessions are a refuge of tranquility. My calm demeanor provides a steady presence, allowing you to explore your thoughts and emotions at your own pace, free from the pressures of the outside world.

Directness: I believe in open and honest communication. I will gently guide our conversations, helping you uncover insights and identify patterns in your life. My direct approach is aimed at fostering self-awareness and facilitating meaningful change.

Engagement: Your well-being is my top priority, and I am fully engaged in our sessions. I actively listen to your concerns and respond with empathy and insight. Together, we will explore your inner world, working collaboratively to achieve your therapeutic goals.

Non-Judgmental: In my practice, there is no room for judgment. I approach every client with an open heart and an open mind. Your experiences, thoughts, and feelings are met with acceptance and understanding, free from any judgment or criticism.

Compassion: Compassion is the cornerstone of my therapeutic philosophy. I deeply care about your journey and am committed to helping you navigate life's challenges with kindness and support. Together, we will work towards healing and personal growth.

Empathy: I strive to understand your perspective fully. I empathize with your experiences and emotions, helping you feel heard and validated. Through empathy, we can build a strong therapeutic relationship that promotes trust and healing.

In our work together, you can expect a therapeutic experience that is tailored to your unique needs and goals. I am here to walk alongside you on your journey, offering guidance, support, and insight as you explore the depths of your inner world.

Thank you for considering me as your therapist. I look forward to the opportunity to work with you, support your growth, and help you find greater fulfillment and contentment in your life.
